Environments 1 assignment

Environments 1 wasn't exactly my favourite course, to be quite frank. I've never been very artistic or good at making anything look good, whether in 2D or 3D, so taking this course as an elective felt like probably one of the dumbest mistakes I could have made going into my second year at DAE. Every assignment always felt like a struggle, and in the beginning, I had no clue what I was supposed to be doing. However, I do think now, looking back, that even though I'm still not nearly as good at making visually appealing 3D assets as some of the artists who took this course, I feel like I've learned a lot about the process that artists have to go through to create game-ready assets. More importantly, it has made me more aware of how every aspect of a game is made, not just the programming part. It also makes it easier for me to talk with artists about the vision for a game to make sure we're all on the same wavelength.
