PZ Antwerp

At the end of my secondary school career I had to do a ~4 week internship which i did at the police department of my home town of Antwerp. This was mainly possible because the Antwerp police department has quite a large IT department that develops it's own in house application called "FOCUS" amongst other tasks like developing small applications using microsoft powerapps to quickly fulfill the needs of the police force short-term. I worked on the latter developing a small application in collaboration with my mentor at the company. Looking back at it it was a fun challenge and something completely different from what I had done before but Microsoft Powerapps does have it's limitations that make it less interesting for developing things outside the microsoft ecosystem or just about any larger scale applications amongst others. So although interesting it isn't something I can see myself working with again in the near-future.
